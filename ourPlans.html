Common questions

What type of task could I send?

You can sedn any type of task, for example workflows, setting new notifications, creating a new form, refreshing your webpage, or change pipelines from services or sales.Nevertheless, this depends on the package you selected.

What Hubspot services we cover?

We cover all the Hubspot environment, we can acompany from Onboarding, helping your company to set and understand the process on Hubspot, to create new processes and optimize the old processes you have. On the other hand, we also cover all the Hubspot Hubs, sales, marketing and services.

Why hire an Onboarding service?

Because you don't have enough time to learn or set up your marketing, sales and services processes, since you need to be focused on your business. This is why, is better to ensure this process through experts who get the most out of Hubspot and your sales method.

Does my team also needs to work on Hubspot?

We will perform all the automation and set up processes on Hubspot for you. Nevertheless, we will need from you and your team some feedback and ideas that they can give us, in order to create the perfect CRM for your business and boost it to another level.

How long does an Onboarding process take?

The Onboarding process does not have an established time, since every marketing, sales and services process is different in each company. Therefore, it is necessary to make a unique schedule in each Onboarding process.

Don't worry, we can manage Wordpress too and make integrations with Hubspot, in order to send and receive information. We can also install or embed codes for CTA's or forms and more.

Do you provide content?

No, apologies, Merkub is not a marketing agency, we charge for your repetitive activities to give you time for the important things in your company.

No, as said before, Merkub is not a marketing agency, so we only take care of your repetitive tasks.
